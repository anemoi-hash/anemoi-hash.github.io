Anemoi: a Family of ZK-friendly AO Hash Functions.

Anemoi is part of the Arithmetization-Oriented primitives designed for advanced cryptographic protocols such as Zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs of knowledge.
More precisely, Anemoi is a new family of ZK-friendly permutations, that can be used to construct efficient hash functions and compression functions.

For more details, please find bellow the following resources:
  • our paper on ePrint: https://eprint.iacr.org/2022/840.
  • our Git repository: https://github.com/vesselinux/anemoi-hash/.
